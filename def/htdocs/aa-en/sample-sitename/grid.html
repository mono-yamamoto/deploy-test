Grid System

Divided the entire area into 12 grids. Gutters (intervals) of 15px in PC and 5px in SP*1 are set on both sides of each grid.
.col-＜Device＞-＜Number of Grids＞ is nested in .row.
＜Device＞ is "lg" for PC and "sm" for SP.
＜Number of Grids＞ is from 1 to 12.

Basically, .row is nested in .container.
If you set a horizontal belt with background color, please nest .container in .container-fluid.

*1 SP means "Smart Phone".

Basic Class Modifier What to Change
.col-lg-＜Number of Grids＞ .col-lg-pull-＜Number of Grids＞ In reverse order (PC).
.col-lg-push-＜Number of Grids＞ In reverse order (PC).
.col-lg-offset-＜Number of Grids＞ To offset grid columns (PC).
.col-sm-＜Number of Grids＞ - -
